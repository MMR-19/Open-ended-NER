"album": A collection of recorded music tracks released as a unified work.
"award": A formal recognition given for achievement or excellence in music.
"band": A group of musicians performing or producing music collectively.
"country": A nation referenced in relation to music production, performance, or culture.
"event": A musical occasion or gathering where performances or ceremonies occur.
"location": A geographical place relevant to musical activities, venues, or origins.
"musical artist": An individual engaged in creating, performing, or producing music.
"musical instrument": A device or tool used to produce musical sounds.
"music genre": A category that classifies music by style, form, or cultural origin.
"organisation": An institution, company, or entity involved in music production, promotion, or distribution.
"person": An individual referenced within the musical context who may not be an artist.
"song": A musical composition that includes vocals, melody, and lyrics.
